TCO ANALYSIS - AWS vs AZURE vs ON-PREMISE

AWS FREE TIER:
- $200 credit for new accounts
- 750 hours of t2.micro monthly
- After free tier: ~$8-10/month

AZURE B1s INSTANCE:
- $7.59/month (pay-as-you-go)
- $5.11/month (1-year savings plan)
- $3.42/month (3-year savings plan)
- $200 credit for new accounts

ON-PREMISE SERVER:
- Hardware: $1500 one-time
- Power: $20/month
- Internet: $60/month
- Maintenance: $80/month
- Total 3-year cost: $6780

CONCLUSION:
Cloud services reduce initial costs and provide scalability.
